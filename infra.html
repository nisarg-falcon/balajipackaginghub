Infrastructure



BALAJI PACKAGING is one of India’s largest manufacturers of folding cartons, and India’s largest standalone converter of paperboard. BALAJI PACKAGING manufactures folding cartons, printed blanks and outers, litho-lamination, plastic cartons, blister packs and shelf-ready packaging. BALAJI PACKAGING has also ventured into the flexible packaging industry, with capability to produce printed cork-tipping paper, laminates, sleeves and wrap-around labels.
